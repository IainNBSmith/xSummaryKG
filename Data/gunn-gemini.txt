
Police arrested two people sleeping in a stolen truck in Gunn, Alberta. The truck was reported stolen in Lac Ste. Anne County in November. The man, Kevin Stolth, has outstanding warrants and was charged with possessing stolen property and breaching release conditions. The woman, Tanya Westoby, also has warrants and was charged with possessing stolen property and breaching probation. Stolth is in custody, while Westoby was released with a court date.
