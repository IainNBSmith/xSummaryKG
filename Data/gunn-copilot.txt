Two individuals, Kevin Daniel Stolth and Tanya Nichol Westoby, were arrested by the Parkland RCMP and the Crime Reduction Unit in Gunn on January 26. They were found sleeping in a stolen Ford pickup truck. Stolth, who had five outstanding warrants, is now facing additional charges for possessing illegally obtained property worth over $5,000 and failing to comply with release conditions. Westoby, also with five warrants, was charged similarly and for breaching probation. Stolth remains in custody and is due in court on February 5, while Westoby was released and is scheduled for court on February 21.
